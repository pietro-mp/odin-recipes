Corn cake with guava paste

Description

Simple recipe of a brazilian corn cake with guava paste, famous for it's simplicity and taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornmeal
  • 1/2 cup corn flakes
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
  • Guava paste (goiabada), cut into small cubes

Steps

  1. In a bowl, sift together the cornmeal, all-purpose flour, sugar, and yeast. Then add the corn flakes.
  2. In a separate container, mix well the milk, eggs, and oil.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix well.
  4. Grease a baking pan with oil and dust with cornmeal. Pour in half of the batter and add a layer of cubed guava paste. Then pour in the remaining batter and top with more guava paste.
  5. Bake at 200°C (about 390°F) for 40 minutes, or until done (baking time may vary depending on the oven).
