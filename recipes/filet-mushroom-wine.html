Filet Mignon with Mushroom and Red Wine Sauce

Description

The best filet with red wine sauce you will ever get.

Ingredients

  • 6 filet mignon steaks
  • 1 small jar of champignon mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 beef bouillon cubes
  • 1 cup dry red wine
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup water (for dissolving the cornstarch)

Steps

  1. Sear the steaks in 2 tablespoons of butter and set them aside.
  2. In the same pan, saut√© the onion, mushrooms, and bouillon cubes in the remaining butter.
  3. Add the red wine and let it boil for about 10 minutes.
  4. Dissolve the cornstarch in 1/4 cup of water and add it to the sauce to thicken.
  5. Return the steaks to the pan, let them simmer in the sauce for 5 minutes, then turn off the heat.
  6. Serve immediately.
